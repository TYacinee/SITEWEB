Dataset Overview

This graphs are interactive visualizations built from real Rocket League match data that I collected to train EVA. These plots help explore performance, playstyle, patterns and help understand what kind of data I am using. Those are some of the visualization that can be done with the data that I have, when I will collect all of my data, they might be more graphs and they might be more representative. Don't hesitate to interact with the graphs and to click on the buttons !

Shooting Efficiency

This scatter plot explores the relationship between the number of shots taken and goals scored per match. Each point represents a match, allowing us to visually assess finishing efficiency. Interactive filters allow comparison between all matches, wins, and losses to better understand how shooting efficiency impacts match outcomes.

Boost Management

This bar chart summarizes how boost is managed across matches by comparing collected, used, and stolen boost. Interactive controls allow switching between total values and per-match averages, helping identify whether boost usage reflects efficient resource management or excessive consumption.

What Drives Winning?

This comparative visualization highlights key performance metrics such as goals, shots, saves, and demos by contrasting winning and losing matches. By examining how these averages differ, the graph reveals which aspects of gameplay are most strongly associated with success, offering insights beyond simple scorelines.
