Interests & motivation

My main interests lie at the intersection of video games, music, and digital culture. I am especially drawn to projects that treat games not only as entertainment, but as rich systems of data, strategies, and behaviors.

Through projects like EVA, I aim to explore how machine learning and data visualization can be transformed into tools of understanding, learning, and critical reflection, rather than opaque black boxes.

EVA in my academic journey

EVA is more than a technical project: it represents my approach to data science. Instead of focusing solely on performance, the project emphasizes interpretability, human-centered design, and pedagogy.

By combining predictive models, SHAP-based explanations, and a conversational interface, EVA acts as a bridge between raw data and meaningful coaching insights.
