EVA ‚Äî Esport Virtual Assistant (v1.0)

EVA is a Rocket League data coach built to help players understand what happened in a match and what to focus on next. The goal is not just to predict the outcome, but to explain the result in a way a real coach would: highlighting the most impactful statistics, comparing them with winning benchmarks, and translating them into actionable advice.

How EVA works (in simple terms)

EVA relies on match-level statistics collected from Ballchasing and organized into a dataset. For any selected match, EVA:

1) Reads your match

You enter a player name, select a match index, and EVA loads the corresponding stats from the dataset.

2) Explains the outcome

EVA uses model explanations (SHAP) to identify which statistics pushed the prediction toward win or loss.

3) Gives coaching advice

EVA returns your top 3 improvement targets and compares them against the average of winning matches to show the gap.

4) You can chat with EVA

After the analysis, you can ask questions and EVA answers like a coach using your match report (no made-up stats).
